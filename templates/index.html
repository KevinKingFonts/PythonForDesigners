Welcome to Python for Designers

This manual is an introduction to Python3 and Drawbot. It covers some language and programming basics as well as a good share of the Drawbot API. If you are looking for a gentle introduction to programming and you are often busy with drawing stuff on a screen, this is maybe the right place for you!

Support

This manual is open-source, free to read and free from ads. There are a couple of ways to support my work other than money. First of all, you can spread the word on social media and help me reach a broader audience. Or, you can help me to organize a Python+Drawbot workshop in your city.

Contribute

If you spot any mistake, maybe something is not entirely clear, or you think something could be explained better, please, open an issue at the project GitHub page. If you are accustomed to git wizardry in general, I also accept to review “pull requests”.

Who's

Python for Designers is an initiative by Roberto Arista. Beatrice Bianchet drew the illustrations, Silvio Lorusso provided editing and feedback. If you want to check a complete list of the technology & people involved in making this website, you can consult the Impressum page.
